Steps to make pasta

  1. Boil pasta in salted water, drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato puree, oregano, salt, and pepper; simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce until evenly coated.
  5. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
